Backgrounds and Borders

    Notes:

  1. border: This can be done in one or multiple lines

    • border: 3px solid red;

      • Same As...

    • border-width: 3px;
      border-color: red;
      border-style: solid;

  2. background-position Can be center center or use PX or other form. Just be aware there are multiple ways to adjust the image inside of the container.

    • background-position: center top;
    • background-position: -100px -100px;

  3. Background may be done in one line as well

    • background: url('./static/images/stars.jpg') no-repeat center center/cover;

      • Same As...

    • background-image: url('./static/images/stars.jpg');
      background-repeat: no-repeat;
      background-position: center top;
      background-size: cover;

  4. Transparent background image (PNG)

    • background: url('./static/images/leaf.png') no-repeat center center; (centers image)
    • background-attachment: fixed; (fixes image when scrolling)
